About Us

Founded in 2025

We are committed to providing top-notch online performance and rehearsal booking tools for aspiring artists.

Our Story

Our founder has a passion for the marching arts. She has been involved in music her entire life, especially marching percussion. She wants to share her love for her section and performance with the world, and ensure everyone has the same love she does.

Our Mission

Bring a love of drumming and percussion to a wider audience, and allow them to practice on their own terms.
